About BookVault

BookVault is a Django-powered digital library built to showcase the beauty of books. Whether you're a casual reader or a dedicated bibliophile, our platform makes it easy to browse titles, explore author details, and manage your collection.

Built as a learning project, BookVault demonstrates modern web development with Django templates, Tailwind CSS, and a clean, responsive design that works on every device.

  • Responsive design for mobile, tablet, and desktop
  • Full book metadata with ISBN and page counts
  • Django admin for easy content management
